# SAR Tournament Manager

A simple browser-based tournament calculator for Super Animal Royale.

## Features

* Paste `/getplayers` output directly
* Automatic scoring (kills + placement)
* Multiple rounds
* Team grouping system
* Solos and Teams mode
* Final leaderboard with placements
* Custom scoring settings
* Background color & gradient customization
* 
## Notes

* Works entirely in browser (no install needed)
* Data is saved locally

## Made for Super Animal Royale tournaments


## HOW TO WORK: 2 WAYS!

## WAY 1

Step 1: Download the file as ZIP

Step 2: Open the code in notepad

Step 3: Press File.

Step 4: Press Save As

Step 5: Make sure the file is named as: "sar_tournament_manager.html" MAKE SURE THERE IS NO .txt BEHIND IT!

Step 6: Make sure the Save As Type is All files!

Step 7: Go to files on your desktop, and search for "sar_tournament" The file should pop up

Step 8: Right click the file, and press open with Google, Microsoft, etc. ONLY APPS LIKE GOOGLE OR MICROSOFT OR THE APPLICATION WONT WORK!

Step 9: Follow the steps below this

## WAY 2, SIMILAR TO WAY 1:
(using windows 11)

Step 1: Go to the Sar Tournament Calculator Code. 

Step 2: Copy all of the code.

Step 3: Open notepad, and paste the code on it.

Step 4: Press file on notepad

Step 5: Press Save As, and rename the file to "sar_tournament_manager.html" Make sure there is no .txt behind it.

Step 6: Press the Text Documents *(txt) button, and make sure it's set to All Files. If it's not set to all files, then it won't work.

Step 7: Go to your files, and in the home, search up "sar_tournament" and it should pop up.

Step 8: Right click the file, and search for an app like Microsoft, Google, any browser app of your choice. I'm using Microsoft.

Step 9: Follow the steps below.

## HOW TO USE? 

Step 1: Press "Add Tournament" on the website, and name your tournament

Step 2: Make your scoring! 

Step 3: Choose between Solos or Teams. It should be on teams when you open the file in browser, and make sure if you want to do teams, you press solos and then teams again to make it active.

Step 4: Choose if you want to change the color of your background! The color option on the left is for a solid color for the whole background, while the 2 on the left (#color1 and #color2) are for gradient. Hex codes only!

Step 5: After a round of a tournament you host, type /getplayers to get your code! Then, paste it in the text box where it says "Paste /getplayers output here..."

Step 6: Press "Add Round" you will get your scoring

Step 7: Press the group people button, and hover your mouse over someone. Their entire column should turn blue. Click their column, and as many other players are in their team. Press the group people button again to make your team. You have to name the team, so you could ask the people who are participating in your event to make a team name! Otherwise, do team 1, team 2, team 3, etc.

Step 8: Continue doing rounds as needed.

Step 9: Once finished, press the "Finished!" button. This will save your tournament!
